Monuments in Pripyat

Amusement Park

❮ ❯

Pripyat Amusement Park is an abandoned amusement park located in Pripyat, Ukraine.
It was to be inaugurated on May 1, 1986, in time for the May Day celebrations,
but these plans were cancelled on April 26,
when the Chernobyl disaster occurred a few miles away.

Polissya Hotel

❮ ❯

Hotel Polissya is located in the center of the abandoned town of Pripyat.
It was built in the mid-70s to accommodate delegations and guests visiting Pripyat and the Chernobyl power plant.
From the roof you have a beautiful view of the city and its surroundings.


Energetik Palace of Culture

❮ ❯

The Palace of Culture Energetic is an abandoned palace of culture located in the city of Pripyat,
in the exclusion zone of Chernobyl in Ukraine.


Avanhard Stadium

❮ ❯

Avanhard Stadium is an abandoned football stadium in Pripyat, Ukraine.
It was built to become the home stadium of FC Stroitel Pripyat.
Due to the Chernobyl catastrophe, it was never realized.
It is named, like many other reasons, by the union of the Ukraine sports club.
