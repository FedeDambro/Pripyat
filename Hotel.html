Dnipro Hotel

The hotel is located in the very center of the historical and business part of Kyiv, the Dnipro hotel successfully combines classic design and a comfortable atmosphere with everything necessary for a business person.

Comfortable rooms provide rest and a wonderful view of the Dnipro River, and the team of cooks will always delight you with something delicious and exquisite.

The hotel is located 200 metres from the main square of the city - Maidan Nezalezhnosti on the central Khreschatyk Street.

Among the amenities of this property are a 24-hour front desk, room service, and free WiFi throughout the property. The establishment features a spa center, evening entertainment, and family rooms.
