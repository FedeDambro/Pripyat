Location

Pripyat is situated near the border with Belarus, in the north of Ukraine. It was a city close to the central Chernobyl.

Directions

Information on how to visit Pripyat, if permitted, and safety warnings.

From Milan to Pripyat

To reach Pripyat from Milan, the journey begins with a flight to Kiev. Here are the details:

Flight from Milan to Kiev

There are 3 options for going to Kiev:

The best option:

  • Route:

    Take the bus from Milan to Bergamo, then the flight to Iasi, on the border between Romania and Moldava, and finally, after a 2.5 km walk, the bus to Kiev.

  • Duration:

    About 22h20min

  • Price:

    from 64-277€. Depends on the flight.

The fastest:

  • Route:

    Direct flight from Malpensa (Milan) to Suceava, also on the border between Romania and Moldava, and then, after a 17 km taxi ride, the bus to Kiev.

  • Duration:

    About 16h40min

  • Price:

    from 111-291€. Depends on the flight.

The most economical:

  • Route:

    Take the train to Bologna, then the flight to Iasi, and finally, after a 2.5 km walk, the bus to Kiev.

  • Duration:

    About 22h40min

  • Price:

    from 56-435€. Depends on the flight.

From Kiev to Pripyat

After arriving in Kiev, you can continue the journey to Pripyat. Given the restrictions of the area:

  • It is advisable to join a guided tour, which often includes transportation from Kiev.
  • Always check safety regulations and access requirements before visiting.
