1. Tour

Information

Following a predefined tour to visit Pripyat, it is advisable to visit:

  • Abandoned Zalissya village
  • Huge secret radar “Duga-1” and the Chernobyl-2 town
  • The almost completely buried village of Kopachi and the still-standing kindergarten
  • Chernobyl Nuclear Power Plant covered by the new safe confinement - the arch
  • The NPP cooling pond with giant catfish
  • The Red Forest
  • The firefighters' barracks
  • The city of Pripyat
